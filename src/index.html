Who We Are

Empowering QA Professionals Across Bangladesh

BAQC is a non-profit community initiative dedicated to raising the standard of software quality assurance in Bangladesh.

We believe in the power of community-driven learning, sharing knowledge, and fostering a culture of excellence. Whether you are a fresh graduate or a seasoned expert, BAQC is your platform to grow.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BAQC?

BAQC (Bangladesh Aspiring QA Community) is a non-profit initiative dedicated to building a quality future for Bangladesh by empowering QA professionals through mentorship, workshops, and networking.

How can I join BAQC?

You can join by filling out our registration form and joining our Discord community. It's the best way to stay updated on events and resources.

Is there a fee to join the community?

No, membership is completely free. We believe in accessible knowledge sharing for everyone.

How do I get the personal subdomain?

Registered members who actively build their portfolio can request a personalized username.baqc.bd subdomain to showcase their skills to the world.

Who can attend workshops?

Our workshops are open to everyone, from students to seasoned professionals, regardless of their background.

How can I be a volunteer?

We welcome passionate individuals! Reach out to us via email at contact@baqc.bd or drop a message in our community group to express your interest.
